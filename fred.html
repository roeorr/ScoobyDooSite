Fred Jones

Profile

Fred is what most would consider the leader of the Mystery Team Fred always looks out for Shaggy and Scooby now matter what trouble the find themselves in. Fred usually drives the Mystery Machine whenever the gang is on their way to check out a haunted place.

Fred also is said to have a crush on Daphne! Many of the episodes on TV depict this lust he has for her but it is never truly confirmed.
